Alfredo Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200g fettuccine
  • 100g butter
  • 150g Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Cooking Instructions

  1. Cook the fettuccine according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, melt the butter and saut√© the garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add the heavy cream and bring to a simmer.
  4. Stir in the Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth.
  5. Toss the cooked fettuccine in the sauce, season with salt and pepper, and serve.

Serving Suggestions

Serve with a sprinkle of extra Parmesan and a side of garlic bread.
